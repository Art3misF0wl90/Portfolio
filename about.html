About

My name is Rory Weldon, and I'm a computer science senior at Kennesaw State University. The thing that drives me to build is wanting to understand how something works all the way down, not just enough to make it run. I tend to design before I code and structure before I build, so I get the structure right instead of untangling a mess I made by rushing.

My builds reflect that. My stock prediction project is going through a full refactor because the first version stopped making sense, and shipping something I understood mattered more than shipping fast.

Outside of code, I do a lot of worldbuilding; for TTRPG campaigns and a fantasy novel I'm writing, which comes from the same place: wanting to build a system with rules and see what comes out of it.
